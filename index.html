About Us

Welcome to Nickel City 9 Baseball, established in 2012, dedicated to advancing and honing young athletes' talents from their earliest steps onto the diamond. With a passion for player development, we have been fortunate enough to have a guiding hand in numerous college and professional careers.

Our journey began with the goal of teaching my sons how to play the game the right way. Over the years, we have proudly witnessed many of our players evolve into skilled competitors, thriving both on and off the field.
