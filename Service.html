Services & Policies

Below are the services currently offered by Nickel City Nine. Additionally, all events and updates will be regularly posted on our social media pages to keep players and families informed about schedules, achievements, and more!

Individual Instruction:

One-on-one coaching sessions focusing on specific skills and techniques

Group Instruction:

Small group sessions designed to work on specific skills in a group / team setting

Team Instruction:

Our NC9 Training Staff will execute tailored programs to ensure your team receives high-level training. We're committed to helping your team succeed with our training approach.

Travel Teams:

Competitive travel teams for players looking to compete regionally or nationally, emphasizing skill development and tournament play.

Mini Camps:

Short-duration camps focused on intensive skill development over a few days, ideal for honing specific techniques.

Summer Camps:

Extended camps during summer months provide a complete training experience including infield / outfield training, baserunning, hitting, situational training, throwing mechanics, and skills competitions.

Lesson Package Policy

Effective September 2, 2024, Nickel City Nine Baseball LLC. will introduce new lesson package options for our clients. All weekly lessons will be available exclusively in packages of 3, 5, and 10 sessions.
Cancellation Policy:
To ensure smooth scheduling and respect for our instructors' time, we kindly request that cancellations be made at least 8 hours in advance of the scheduled lesson. Failure to cancel within this timeframe without special notice will result in a charge to your account for the lesson. We understand that unforeseen circumstances may arise, and we appreciate your cooperation in adhering to this policy. Thank you for your understanding and continued support.
