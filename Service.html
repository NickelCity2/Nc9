Lesson Package Policy

Effective September 2, 2024, Nickel City Nine Baseball LLC. will introduce new lesson package options for our clients. All weekly lessons will be available exclusively in packages of 3, 5, and 10 sessions.
Cancellation Policy:
To ensure smooth scheduling and respect for our instructors' time, we kindly request that cancellations be made at least 8 hours in advance of the scheduled lesson. Failure to cancel within this timeframe without special notice will result in a charge to your account for the lesson. We understand that unforeseen circumstances may arise, and we appreciate your cooperation in adhering to this policy. Thank you for your understanding and continued support.
